Who We Are

Afterhrs Was Born
After Midnight

AFTERHRS started as a sketchbook kept open past 2 AM. A collection of ideas about what clothes should feel like — not loud, not ordinary, not made for daylight.

We design for the quiet hours. For the moments between midnight and 4 AM when everything feels more honest. Our pieces are built to move, to breathe, to last — and to feel like they belong to you the moment you wear them.

Every fabric is sourced with intention. Every silhouette is drawn by hand. We are a small brand that believes clothing is not just what you wear — it is how you carry yourself through the world.
